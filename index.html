Based in the wonderful Correzian landscape of south western France, Cath Neal is a British landscape archaeologist and stained glass artist who first studied the craft in 2005. Taking her inspiration from nature, people and landscapes, Cath crafts bespoke pieces and welcomes commissions, small and large. If you have an enquiry please send Cath an email with details/sketches/photos and she will endeavor to get back to you within 48 hours to discuss further.
